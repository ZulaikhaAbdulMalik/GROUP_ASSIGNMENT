FAQ

How do I sign up as a member?

You can choose whether you want to sign up via online or offline. You can go to our nearest store and ask the staff to sign you up for for the membership include pay the membership directly. You also can sign up via online by browsing our official website at the membership section.

What are the benefits of signing up?

After signing up, you can easily get 30% discount on any puchased items. You will also get a discount of RM 3 for delivery from 3 puchase or above This discount are also applied in your nearest SEES Kitchenware store. Every purchase you made will be given 300 point so you can redeem some gift.

How much does it cost to use sign up membership?

RM 10 for one person. You can pay this membership ehether it is via online or offline.

What are the payment options available?

Payment can be accept in cash where you need to find our nearest store. There is also an option to pay via online. We accept debit card, credit card or online banking or online transfer

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can just go to the nearest store and ask the staff to end your membership. You can also contact us via social media that are provided in the support section.

How long does it take to deliver my order?

For Sabah and Sarawak, it takes 3-4 days to shipped out the items and will be recieved after 2-3 weeks depending on our courier. For Peninsular Malaysia, the items will be shipped out 3-4 days and also be recieved after 2-3 weeks.
