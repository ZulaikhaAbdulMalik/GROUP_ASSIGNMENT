History

SEES Kitchenware is an online retail store that sells a wide range of quality cookware, bakeware, glassware, and other kitchen and living products at affordable prices. We are based in Malaysia and have been in the industry for over 10 years. They cater to both household and commercial customers, and offer fast delivery and secure payment options. You can find more information about us on our official website or any of our social media.

SEES Kitchenware started as a wholesale supplier of kitchenware products for restaurants and hotels.













We realized that there was a demand for quality kitchenware products among the general public, who wanted to cook like professional chefs at home. We decided to open our online store in 2020, and since then, we have been providing customers with the best products and service possible.

We also have our own brand of products, such as dish racks, clothes hangers, thermos bottles, water bottles, etc. We offer free shipping for orders over RM150.00 and a 10% discount for new buyers. We also have a loyalty program that rewards customers with points and vouchers.
